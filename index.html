Downsize to Happy

Over the last few years, I've become obsessed with the idea of downsizing to focus on what truly brings happiness in life. This site aims to share some methods for downsizing, and reasons you should try it too.

Start your journey by checking out Marie Kondo's website. I would also take a look at Cladwell if you're interested in creating and using a capsule wardrobe (though I haven't tried the site yet.)
